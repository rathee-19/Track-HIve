Top Artists

Drake

Drake, original name Aubrey Drake Graham, (born October 24, 1986, Toronto, Ontario, Canada), Canadian rap musician who first gained fame as an actor on the acclaimed TV teenage drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation and went on to a successful and influential music career.

KK

Born in Delhi on 23 August 1968 to Malayali parents, Krishnakumar Kunnath grew up in New Delhi. He sang 3,500 jingles before breaking into Bollywood. KK attended Delhi's Mount St Mary's School, and Kirori Mal College, Delhi University.

Kanye west

Kanye Omari West was born on June 8, 1977, in Atlanta, Georgia. After his parents divorced when he was three years old he moved with his mother to Chicago, Illinois. His father, Ray West, is a former Black Panther and was one of the first black photojournalists at The Atlanta Journal-Constitution.

Top Albums

Graduation

Graduation" is the third album by Kanye West,
released in 2007. It was successful
commercially and critically acclaimed for its
electro-pop and synth-heavy sound. The album
debuted at number one on the US
Billboard 200 chart and was nominated
for several Grammy Awards.

Take Care

"Take Care" is Drake's second album,
released in 2011. It received critical acclaim
for its introspective lyrics and atmospheric
production, and was a commercial success.
The album debuted at number one on the
US Billboard 200 chart and won the Grammy
Award for Best Rap Album in 2013.

Pal

"Pal" is a 2018 studio album by
Indian singer KK. The album features
romantic tracks with lyrics by
Manoj Muntashir and music by
various composers.
It received positive reviews for KK's
vocal performance and the album's
musical arrangements. Popular tracks include
"Hud Se," "Ishq Mubarak," and the
title track "Pal."
